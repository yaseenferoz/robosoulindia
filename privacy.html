Privacy Policy

We take your privacy seriously and are committed to protecting your personal information. This policy describes what information we collect, how it is used, and how it is protected.

How We Use Your Information
  • To respond to your inquiries and provide customer service.
  • To send you marketing and promotional materials that we think you may be interested in.
  • To improve our website and services based on your feedback and usage data.
Information Security

We take all necessary steps to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. We use appropriate security measures such as SSL encryption, firewalls, and secure passwords to protect your data.

We may use third-party services such as Google Analytics and Mailchimp to collect and process your information. These services have their own privacy policies and terms of service, and we encourage you to review them.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, and delete your personal information. You may also opt-out of receiving our marketing and promotional materials at any time.

Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the updated policy on our website.
